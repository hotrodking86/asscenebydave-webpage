About Dave

Hi, I'm Dave, the creator of As Scene by Dave.

I've always had a passion for history, especially Hollywood history. In my early twenties, I developed an interest in visiting cemeteries and historic sites throughout Southern California. What started as a personal hobby quickly became a way for me to connect with the stories of the people and places that helped shape Hollywood and American culture.

For years, I explored these locations simply for my own enjoyment. I would visit gravesites, historic homes, filming locations, and lesser-known landmarks without ever thinking about recording or sharing the experience.

I've also spent years giving informal driving tours of Los Angeles to family and friends, sharing the fascinating history behind famous landmarks, Hollywood legends, and forgotten stories that many people drive past without ever noticing.

Eventually, I realized there were others who shared the same curiosity and appreciation for these places. That's what inspired me to start As Scene by Dave.

Through my videos, I bring viewers along as I explore historic locations, visit the final resting places of notable figures, uncover true crime stories, and share the rich history that makes Los Angeles and Hollywood such unique places.

Whether you're interested in classic Hollywood, true crime, famous filming locations, or hidden pieces of local history, I hope you'll enjoy joining me on these journeys.

Thank you for visiting and for being part of the adventure.

— Dave
