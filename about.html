About Dave

Hey, I’m Dave. I love finding interesting places and learning the stories behind them. From Hollywood landmarks and celebrity graves to true crime locations and forgotten pieces of history, I’m always looking for places with a story worth telling. As Scene by Dave started as a way to share those discoveries with others. If you’ve ever wondered what happened at a certain location or what a place looks like today, you’re in the right spot Thanks for stopping by, and I hope you enjoy exploring these stories with me. — Dave
